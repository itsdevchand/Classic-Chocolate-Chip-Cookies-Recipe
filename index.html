Classic Chocolate Chip Cookies

The ultimate soft, chewy, and perfectly golden chocolate chip cookies. A timeless favorite that's easy to make!

Prep Time: 15 mins Cook Time: 10 mins Servings: 24 cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter, softened
  • ¾ cup granulated sugar
  • ¾ cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 ¼ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat and Prepare: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line two large baking sheets with parchment paper.
  2. Wet Ingredients: In a large bowl, beat the softened butter, granulated sugar, brown sugar, and vanilla extract until the mixture is smooth and creamy.
  3. Add Eggs: Beat in the eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition.
  4. Dry Ingredients: In a separate bowl, combine the flour, baking soda, and salt. Gradually blend this dry mixture into the creamed butter mixture.
  5. Fold in Chocolate: Stir in the chocolate chips by hand using a spatula until they are evenly distributed.
  6. Scoop: Drop rounded tablespoons of dough onto the prepared baking sheets, leaving about 2 inches of space between each cookie.
  7. Bake: Bake for 9 to 11 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown. The centers should still look slightly soft.
  8. Cool: Cool on the baking sheets for 2 minutes, then transfer the cookies to wire racks to cool completely.
